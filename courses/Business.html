BUSINESS LANGUAGE

By: Prof Ali

Tailored to industry-specific vocabulary and communication skills. Focus on professional setting, meeting, emails, and presentation. Common for languages like English, Mandarin, Turkish, German etc...

A Business Language course focuses on equipping students with the communication skills necessary for professional environments. The course covers essential business vocabulary, formal writing styles, and presentation techniques. Students learn to write emails, reports, and proposals, adapting their language to various workplace scenarios. It also emphasizes effective verbal communication in meetings, negotiations, and networking. The course may include cross-cultural communication, preparing students to work in global business settings. Practical exercises like role-playing and simulations enhance learning. Business language courses often cater to different industries, such as finance, marketing, or management. This training boosts confidence in professional interactions. Additionally, students acquire skills in persuasive communication and conflict resolution. Ultimately, the course aims to make students more effective communicators in business contexts.
