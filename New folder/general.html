GENERAL LANGUAGE

By: Miss Beaula

Focus on reading , writing , speaking , and listening. Suitable for beginners , intermediate ,
and advance.
EXAMPLE: Standard English/French/German Classes

A general language course is designed to improve proficiency in a specific language. It
typically covers listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills. The course may focus
on grammar, vocabulary, and pronunciation to build a solid foundation. Learners are introduced
to cultural nuances and idiomatic expressions of the language. It is suitable for beginners or
intermediate learners looking to expand their knowledge. Interactive activities like role-playing
or discussions help improve communication skills. The course may include exercises for enhancing
reading comprehension and writing ability. Teachers provide personalized feedback to ensure progress.
Online and in-person options are available. Successful completion can lead to improved fluency and
confidence in using the language.
