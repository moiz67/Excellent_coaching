CONVERSATIONAL LANGUAGE

By: Prof Dawood

Emphasis on speaking and listening. Designed to build confidence in real-life conversations.
Ideal for travelers , expats, social use.

Conversational language focuses on improving everyday communication skills. It emphasizes
practical phrases, vocabulary, and expressions used in real-life situations. The goal is to help
learners speak confidently and naturally in casual conversations. This type of language learning
often prioritizes listening and speaking over reading or writing. It includes understanding slang,
idioms, and regional dialects. Conversational language courses encourage active participation,
often through role-playing and group discussions. It helps learners build fluency and reduce
hesitation when speaking. Pronunciation and tone are important to ensure clear communication.
